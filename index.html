About Me

Hello! It’s great to see you here.
You can call me Lola. I’m a data enthusiast with a passion for turning messy data into meaningful stories and actionable insights.

I completed the CUNY Tech Prep Data Science Program while graduating from Baruch College in NYC with a degree in Data Analytics.

Beyond data science and storytelling, I’m also interested in cybersecurity, especially exploring how predictive modeling can enhance threat detection and prevention.

As a first-generation college graduate and woman in tech, I’m committed to giving back to my community and increasing opportunities for underrepresented minorities in STEM.
